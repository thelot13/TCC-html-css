The Clothing Club

Everyone's wardrobe

The Clothing Club is a fully volunteer-based, non-profit "clothing library", where members borrow clothes, use them, and return them to the shared closet for others to borrow. We provide a sustainable, local and inclusive alternative to ’fast fashion’. The purpose of The Clothing Club is to be a shared closet and a diverse community that centers around clothing, fashion, sustainability, and having fun! Our purpose is also to learn from and educate each other on these questions by having an active, open community in the club, online, and through free events, free workshops etc. Everybody, regardless gender, ethnicity, and age are welcome to join the community.
